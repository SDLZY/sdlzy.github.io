Biography

Zhenyang Li is currently a PhD candidate in School of Computer Science and Technology at Shandong University, under the supervision of Prof. Liqiang Nie. Before that, he received his master degree from University of Chinese Academy of Sciences (UCAS) and his bachelor degree from Shandong University (SDU) in 2019 and 2016, respectively. His works focus on visual question answering and visual commonsense reasoning.
